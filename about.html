Who We Are
Crafting Beauty
Since 1985

SIMPLON's Beauty Care Centre was founded in 1985 with a singular vision — to make premium beauty products accessible to every woman in Pakistan. What began as a humble cosmetics shop in Karachi has grown into a beloved multi-city brand.

Over the decades, we have built lasting relationships with our customers by staying true to our promise: exceptional quality, honest pricing, and a warm, welcoming experience at every store.

"Beauty is not about being perfect — it is about feeling confident in your own skin."

Today, SIMPLON operates in Karachi, Lahore, Islamabad, and Peshawar — with more cities on the horizon.
